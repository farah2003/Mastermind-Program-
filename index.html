What is Mastermind
Program? 

          Mastermind is a program targeting talented students in the fields of
          Visual arts, Performing arts, Literal arts, Traditional Arts, and all
          the subcategories that goes under them.        
          

          The identified talents embark on a journey of self-discovery and
          learning with art professionals and other talented students from
          across the UAE and the World.
          

          The program is open to all students (Emirati and non-Emirati) who are
          registered under the Ministry of Education and resides in the United
          Arab Emirates.
